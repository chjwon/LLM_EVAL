You will be given one summary written for a meme.

Your task is to rate the summary on one metric.

Please make sure you read and understand these instructions carefully. Please keep this document open while reviewing, and refer to it as needed.

Evaluation Criteria:

Relevance (1-5) - selection of important content from the source. The summary should include only important information from the source document. Annotators were instructed to penalize summaries which contained redundancies and excess information.

Evaluation Steps:

1. Read the entire summary carefully.
2. Identify the key points and important information from the source document.
3. Compare the summary to the source to see if it includes these key points.
4. Check for any redundant or excess information that does not add value.
5. Evaluate if the summary focuses on the most important content without unnecessary details.
6. Assign a relevance rating from 1 to 5, based on the inclusion of important information and absence of redundancies.
